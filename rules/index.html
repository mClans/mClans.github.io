Rules Updated: February 18th, 2015


A.) General Composure

  • Maintain a pleasant, casual atmosphere. Be respectful and use common sense. Excessive swearing, rudeness and harassment is not tolerated. - kick ban
  • Try to maintain proper spelling and grammar. - warn
  • Do not spam. - kick
  • Do not link to any of the following: - ban
    1. Objectionable material, which includes but is not limited to pornography, "shock sites", viruses, phishing sites and hate groups.
    2. Other websites or servers in a manner that constitutes advertising.
    3. Personal identifying information of other users.
  • Do not encourage rule breaking on the server. - kick ban
  • Do not impersonate any member of the staff, or claim to be a staff member. - kick ban
  • Report issues or bugs to the staff members (mclans.github.io/staff); exploitation of bugs for any reason other than to demonstrate at the request of a staff member will result in a punishment. - kick ban
  • Ban evasion is not permitted and will result in a permanent ban on all accounts associated with the original Minecraft account. - ban

    • B.) Server Behavior

  • Do not send messages that encourage other players into disconnecting from the server. - kick
  • Unless you are explaining the report process, you may not encourage others to report a specific player. - warn kick
  • Please do not publicly accuse any players of hacking. If you believe a player is hacking or breaking any rules report them using the "/report" command. - kick
  • You may not use any form of in game chat to solicit. Any account that is determined to be for the sole use of soliciting will be permanently banned without warning. - ban
  • Your username must be appropriate, and you may be asked to use a different account if your username is found to be inappropriate. (This does not count as ban evasion.) - ban
  • Please use an appropriate skin - you may be punished for an inappropriate skin. - kick ban
  • Threatening users (DDoS, 'Swatting' etc.) is not permitted on our servers. - ban

    • C.) General Gameplay

  • Players may not abuse glitches. - kick ban
  • Players may not prolong a match by avoiding combat and/or hiding in places that are inaccessible or difficult to get to. - kick

    • D.) Client Modifications

  • Players may not use any client modifications that give an unfair advantage over vanilla Minecraft users. - ban
  • Players may not use any external tools that modify gameplay to give an unfair advantage over users not using the tool. - ban

    • E.) Team Griefing

  • Team griefing is prohibited, and is defined as any reckless action that harms your own team, or actions that intentionally harms one's own team. - kick ban
